Spaghetti Recipe

The Ultimate Spaghetti Recipe for MEN

Ingredients:

  • 16oz of Tomato Sauce
  • raw spaghetti noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork (or turkey if you're not a fan)
  • Your favorite veggies (lets be reasonable)
  • Water. Lots.

Items Needed:

  1. Pot(not that..)
  2. Giant spoon
  3. Something to boil water in

Steps:

Just boil some hot water...doesn't matter how much I guess...as long as you can cook all of the spaghetti

Then, just throw them noodles in the pot (make a bowling motion when you do it for better taste results)

Dont worry about cooking the meat. We eat it raw.

Drain the water after about 10 minutes...maybe 12...yeah, 12 is good.

Then, throw that plain ol' tomato sauce into the spaghetti with the raw meat. BOOM! Yer dun.
