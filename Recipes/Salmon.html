Maryland-style Salmon

There is no better way to make Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Big ol' salmon fillet (with or without skin)
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • 2 Lemons
  • Salt/li>
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil (not extra-virgin)

Items Needed:

  1. Large pan
  2. Aluminum foil
  3. An oven

Steps:

Preheat over to 400 degrees

Slap that salmon fillet under some cold water and wash it off

Add the lemon juice before adding the seasonings.

Cover the salmon in Old Bay, and be messy about it!

Gently...place aluminum foil in a pan and add a generous amount of olive oil to the bottom

Now, slap that salmon onto the pan and violently THROW that sucker into the oven.

Wait between 12-15 minutes....turn off the oven, and grab the fish with your bare hands.

DONE!
