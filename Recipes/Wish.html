Wishandwhich

My heart goes out to anyone who needs this...

Ingredients:

  • White Bread
  • Optional: Whatever you have left in your fridge or cabinet

Items Needed:

  1. A negative balance in the bank
  2. Intense Hunger
  3. Imagination

Steps:

Put a slice of bread in each hand.

Close your eyes

Slowly place one slice of bread on top of the other while imagining your favorite ingredients falling on top of it. (dont open your eyes yet)

Add sugar, or salt, or mayonaise, or whatever you think would help *enhance* its flavor

DONE! Now you can open your eyes...
